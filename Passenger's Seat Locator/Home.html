About Us

Since 2017, The bus Company name"#" traverses the Davao Region with routes from Davao to Tagum, New Bataan, Monkayo, Digos, Bansalan, Malita and Laak. Our 170 buses are air-conditioned and non-airconditioned ranging from 18 to 58 seaters. We have 600 employees ensuring that passengers are given a Maayong Pagsakay experience.

Our Vision

To be the most admired transport service company in Mindanao.

Our Mission

We, competent and values-oriented workforce, provide excellent customer service through timely, comfortable, safe and affordable ride. We shall contribute to the development of the community by ushering new and innovative opportunities

Our Values

Teamwork, respect, integrity, innovation and passion.
